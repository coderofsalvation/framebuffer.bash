Framebuffer.bash

framebuffer in bash to draw ascii pixels, borders, animate e.g. (bash ui bootstrap)

Download as .zip Download as .tar.gz View on GitHub

Why

For minimalists and productivity fanatics. The console is sometimes 'good enough' compared to fullfledged client/server html/css/javascript ui's.

plugins/Barchart

usage: barchart <x> <y> <height> "0,1,2,0,2,1,"

The barchart plugin lets you create 3-state barcharts with ease: lets compose our bashscript:

repo="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/coderofsalvation/framebuffer.bash/master"
curl "$repo/framebuffer.bash"       > barchart
curl "$repo/plugins/barchart.bash" >> barchart

lets test a barchart:

source barchart
export WIDTH=22
barchart 20 2 20 "0,1,2,2,1,2,1,0,1,2,0"
barchart 20 4 20 "0,1,2,1,0,1,2,0"

this will produce something like this:
