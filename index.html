Framebuffer.bash

framebuffer in bash to draw ascii pixels, borders, animate e.g. (bash ui bootstrap)

Download as .zip Download as .tar.gz View on GitHub

Why

For minimalists and productivity fanatics. The console is sometimes 'good enough' compared to fullfledged client/server html/css/javascript ui's.

plugins/Barchart

will produce something like this:

stock:  ——▄▄▀▀——▀▀▄▄——▄▄▀▀▄▄———

rates:  __▄▄██__██▄▄__▄▄██▄▄___

redis:  ▀▀▄▄▀▀▄▄▀▀▀▀———————————

by simply using:

put 2 3 "stock:"; echo "0,1,2,0,2,1,0,1,2,1" | barchart 10 2 20 
put 2 5 "rates:"; echo "0,1,2,0,2,1,0,1,2,1" | OFFSET=3 barchart 10 4 20 
put 2 7 "redis:"; redis-cli --csv LRANGE foo 0 -1 | sed 's/[^0-9,\\-]*//g' | barchart 10 6 20
